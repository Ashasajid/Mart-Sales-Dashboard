# Mart-Sales-Dashboard

A Year-over-Year (YoY) interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing $1.6M in retail sales and $175K in profit across North American regions, categories, and shipping modes.

Project Overview: This project involved the creation of a comprehensive Marts Sales Dashboard to analyze retail performance across North America. The dashboard provides a high-level view of key business metrics—Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Delivery times—while allowing for deep dives into regional and categorical trends.

Key Features & Insights:

YoY Performance Tracking: Developed monthly Sales and Profit trends comparing 2019 vs. 2020 to identify seasonal growth patterns.
Regional Analytics: Visualized sales distribution across Central, East, South, and West regions, highlighting the West as a primary revenue driver (33%).
Product & Segment Breakdown: Analyzed performance across three main categories (Office Supplies, Technology, Furniture) and three customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).
Logistics & Operations: Monitored average delivery times and shipping mode preferences, identifying "Standard Class" as the most utilized method.
Financial Insights: Tracked payment mode preferences, showing "Cash on Delivery" (COD) as the leading choice at 43%.

Technical Stack:

Data Visualization: Power BI Desktop.
Data Modeling: DAX for Year-over-Year (YoY) growth calculations and profit margin analysis.
Geospatial Analysis: Integrated map visualizations to track regional sales density.
